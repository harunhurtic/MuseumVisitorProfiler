Museum Visitor Profiler

Developed by Harun Hurtic as part of his Master's Thesis at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU)

How does it work?

This questionnaire helps identify your museum visitor profile based on John H. Falk's Five Visitor Identities. The point values are strategically different so no two profiles will ever add up to the same score.
  • High Scores (6-7) are awarded when the answer strongly and directly aligns with the core motivation and emotional investment of a profile.

  • Medium Scores (4-5) reflect partial alignment, the answer fits but isn’t the most defining aspect for the profile.

  • Low Scores (3) are given when the question/answer only loosely aligns or reflects a behavior not central to the profile’s core motivation.
Even if someone scores highly in multiple categories, one will always be higher. It prevents "middle-of-the-road" results, making sure everyone gets a clear profile.

Read the Master's Thesis for more details into exact score allocation and how it works.
